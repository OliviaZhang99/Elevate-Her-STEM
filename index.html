Real STEM stories for students exploring their future

Elevate Her STEM shares interview responses from women across various STEM fields, including science, technology, engineering, healthcare, education, entrepreneurship, research, and leadership, with the goal of making mentorship more accessible by organizing honest experiences, challenges, and advice in one place.

Why this project is important

Students usually learn about STEM careers in a broad way without knowing the specifics. As such, this project includes detailed advice and the human side of STEM careers.

  • Learn how people with STEM careers first became interested in STEM.
  • Read advice that is honest, practical, and specific.
  • Explore how challenges and failures are a natural part of growth.
  • Browse people by field, career area, and key themes.
