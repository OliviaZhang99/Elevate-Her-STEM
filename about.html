About

As part of a GLOCAL-supported student project, the website contains a list of profiles based on survey responses from women and professionals connected to STEM while emphasizing lived experience, learning, advice, and career reflection.

📋

What we have

Survey responses include specific advice, challenges, and achievements for high school students.

🗂

How it is organized

Each profile has summarized sections so that students can focus on the person's journey and lessons learned.

💡

Why it matters

These real stories help students understand that paths to STEM careers are not always smooth.
