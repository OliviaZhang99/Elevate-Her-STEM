About the Project

As part of a GLOCAL-supported student project, this website contains profiles based on survey responses from women and professionals connected to STEM while emphasizing lived experience, learning, advice, and career reflection.

📋

What we have

Survey responses include specific advice, challenges, achievements, and reflections for high school students.

🗂

How it is organized

Each profile has summarized sections so that students can focus on the person's journey and lessons learned. Empty or missing answer sections are not displayed.

💡

Why it matters

These real stories help students understand that paths to STEM careers are not always smooth, linear, or identical.
